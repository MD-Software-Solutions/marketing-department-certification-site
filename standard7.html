Standard #7 - Marketing and Promotion

The biggest marketing strategy we have for our marketing plan is to work closely with our middle school. We are involved with teacher and student visits to the school to talk about the program, as well as to have DECA meetings with them. This helps bridge the gap between the high school and middle school so that the students are familiar with our programs upon arrival to high school.
