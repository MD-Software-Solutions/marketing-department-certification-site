Marketing Department

About Us

The Johns Creek High School Marketing Department offers students a dynamic learning environment focused on business, marketing, and leadership skills. Through hands-on projects, real-world simulations, and access to professional networks, students gain valuable experience in areas such as advertising, market research, public relations, and sales. The department encourages creativity and critical thinking while preparing students for both college and careers in the marketing field. Additionally, involvement in DECA, a student organization for marketing and entrepreneurship, provides opportunities for competition, networking, and leadership development on both a local and national scale
