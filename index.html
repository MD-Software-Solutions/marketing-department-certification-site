JCHS Marketing Department Overview

The Johns Creek High School Marketing Department offers students a dynamic learning environment focused on business, marketing, and leadership skills. Through hands-on projects, real-world simulations, and access to professional networks, students gain valuable experience in areas such as advertising, market research, public relations, and sales. The department encourages creativity and critical thinking while preparing students for both college and careers in the marketing field.

DECA Involvement and Opportunities

Additionally, involvement in DECA, a student organization for marketing and entrepreneurship, provides opportunities for competition, networking, and leadership development on both a local and national scale.

JCHS Deca

The Marketing Department at Johns Creek High School is dedicated to preparing students with real-world skills and knowledge. Our curriculum includes Marketing Principles, Hospitality Recreation and Tourism, and Digital Marketing, aimed at fostering future industry leaders. Visit us to learn more!
